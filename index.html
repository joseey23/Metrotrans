About us

Why Choose MetroTrans?

At Metrotrans, we understand that access to healthcare goes beyond hospital walls. That’s why we’ve built a service designed to bridge the gap between patients and the care they need.

Our mission is simple: to provide safe, reliable, and dignified non-emergency medical transportation. Whether it’s a routine check-up, dialysis session, rehabilitation appointment, or interfacility transfer, our team ensures every ride is handled with compassion and professionalism.

Our Services

Our Services

Ambulettes

Our Ambulette vehicles are used for passengers that need minimum assistance and can transfer from a wheelchair into a seat of a car.

Wheelchair Vans

Our Wheelchair accessible vans have been modified by increasing the interior size of the vehicle and equipping it with a lift or a ramp to allow wheelchair entry.

Stretcher Vans

Our stretcher vans are used for medical transportation for non-ambulatory or convalescent passengers that need no monitoring while being transported.

Broda

The Broda Traversa Wheelchair is a safe, efficient, and cost-effective transport solution, reducing lifting risks and ideal for single-caregiver operation.
